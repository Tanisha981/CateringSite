About Catering

Tradition since 1889

The Catering was founded in INDIA by Mrs. Smitha. Our delectable food, first class presentation and careful attention to our product and the needs of our clients has resulted in 35 successful years and an excellent reputation among our many happy clients and their guests. Our goal is not simply to make your event a beautiful and joyful experience, but to craft a unique event that will transcend your every expectation.

At our catering, we don’t just make pizza. We make people happy. It was built on the belief that catering night should be special, and we carry that belief into everything we do. With more than 55 years of experience under our belts, we understand how to best serve our customers through tried and true service principles: We create food we’re proud to serve and deliver it fast, with a smile.
